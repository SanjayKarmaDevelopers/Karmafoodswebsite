About Us

Karma Foods – a family run fine dining restaurant focussing on native South Tamilnadu flavours is now a loved name among the locals of Tamilnadu in and around Gandhinagar, Adyar. With a spacious seating capacity of around 60 people, the restaurant’s intricate rural interiors starting with the arduous , time consuming work of mud walls, murals, large wooden windows, all done by artisans brought in from remote villages replicate the native vibe throughout the restaurant.

Our Specalities include Naatukozhi Soup, Nandu Milagu Rasam, Kola Urundai, Naatu Kozhi Idichathu, Mutton Sukka, Karuvatu Kuzhambu, Kothu Parotta from the non-vegetarian and Kalan Milagu, Urulai Roast, Chinna Vengaiyam Poondu Kuzhambu from the vegetarian section.
